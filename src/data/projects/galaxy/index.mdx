## Overview

Galaxy is a SaaS-focused Astro theme with bold visuals, product storytelling sections, and conversion-first page structure.

## Highlights

- Landing page sections built for launches
- Clear visual hierarchy for product messaging
- A layout system that adapts well across screen sizes

## Why it matters

It gives startups a ready-made foundation for presenting their product without starting from a blank page.
