## Overview

Stellar combines a friendly visual style with a flexible content structure for businesses, creators, and personal sites.

## Highlights

- Warm branding and illustration-friendly design
- Blog and business pages in the same theme
- Customizable sections for service and about pages

## Result

The theme works well for people who want something more expressive than a standard corporate layout.
